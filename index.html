About Me

A brief history

My experience with building websites dates back to the 1990s, using GeoCities pages and hard-coding HTML tables into oblivion to awkwardly display information. I sometimes miss the nostalgic, low-functionality, high-personality vibe of Ye Olde Interweb, but I'm glad to say I've learned a lot since then, and the tools available to build things online have vastly improved!
